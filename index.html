🦘 Amazing Kangaroos! 🦘

What is a Kangaroo?

Kangaroos are big, strong animals that live in Australia. They have powerful back legs and love to hop around!

They Can Jump Super High! 🚀

Kangaroos can jump as high as **3 meters (10 feet)** in one leap! That's like jumping over a car! 🚗💨

Baby Kangaroos are Called Joeys 🍼

A baby kangaroo is called a **joey**. It stays inside its mum’s pouch until it’s big enough to hop on its own!

Kangaroos Can’t Walk Backwards! 🚷

Kangaroos can hop forward really fast, but they **can’t walk backwards**. That’s because of their big, strong tails!

They Love to Live in Groups 🦘🦘🦘

Kangaroos live in **big groups called mobs**. A mob can have 50 or more kangaroos all hanging out together!
